The Details About Min Hein Kyaw’s Teeenwear-sweatshirt “Nightmare”

March 13, 2020 - Min Hein Kyaw, the whole king of YUEco 2018-19, a founder of Logic Ent. and Logic Club, joined the first teen-collaboration with Teeenwear.

“TEEEN SØCIETY Co. offered me to Collaborate with me, with as a person Min Hein Kyaw, not as a Logic Club, my Clothing Brand, and I accepted. For a design of sweatshirt, properly we printed a phrase I have liked on the chest and my two tattoo designs on the exact same places where they lie on my body.” he says.

The teeenwear sweatshirt “Nightmare” with a unique fabric and design which reflects its overtly personal artwork created by Min Hein Kyaw is limited 3 sizes; Medium, Large, Extra-Large and 13 qualities each and released on March 13,2020; Friday the 13th, 13:13mins.

“I joined this Collaboration as a partnership for a remembrance and I won’t take any profits from the selling of this product”.he says.

As a return, the Lucky Draw Program is made for Min Hein Kyaw fans which is included the prize like free tea-set fan date with Min Hein Kyaw at Olea Restaurant of Melia Hotel, the five-star graded Hotel in Yangon Myanmar by TEEEN SØCIETY Co.,Ltd.
